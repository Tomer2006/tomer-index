Tomer index

The Tomer index uses a health pillar that is ½ lifespan (life expectancy at birth) and ½ healthspan (WHO HALE—healthy life expectancy), each turned into a 0–1 index with the same 20–85 goalposts, then averaged. Other pillars: GNI per capita (PPP) and safety from intentional homicides /100k. Overall Tomer index: LEIhealth4/9×Income4/9×Safety1/9.
